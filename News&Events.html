The UCU Digital Praxis Innovation Challenge 2022

About this event


The goal of the Challenge is to create an inclusive space for innovation, providing support and funding to UCU students who have “bright ideas”. The UCU Digital Praxis Innovation Challenge 2022 seeks to identify and provide extra support systems to innovative and enterprising University students and guide them through a rigorous technology product prototype sprint from start to finish.
The Challenge seeks to further develop the entrepreneurship, multidisciplinary teamwork and leadership skills of selected student teams while creating a platform for the selected teams to showcase (and sell) their innovations to the public and attract venture capital funding from interested partners.
The winning teams/ideas at the Grand Pitch Event will receive funding of up to 15 Million Uganda Shillings to help them further develop their ideas.
